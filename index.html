REFORESTATION SPREAD THE WORLD VISION FORESTS

Know the source !

Check the products you buy and the companies you support to ensure that your buying habits are not contributing to destruction of habitat elsewhere. Look for labels such as FSC or Rainforest Alliance Certified. Both organizations are committed not only to the conservation of the Earth's resources, but also to advocating for the human rights of the native peoples who inhabit the land many products are sourced from.

It wouldn't be a bad idea to do what a billionaire American did; buying patches of forest land and protecting it from people who want to cut it down! However, privatising natural spaces is not a palatable option for many. Nevertheless, it is possible to showcase natural land as important economic goods through Payment for Ecosystem Services (PES) systems. When money is at stake, perceptions change. It can create renewed interest in protecting land resources.
